## Short tagline (up to 30 chars)

## Subtitle (up to 80 chars)

## Short description (up to 200 chars)

Orca is the proposal CRM built for people with no time to waste. Create professional proposals, share a link with your client, and track opens, accepts and signatures — all in one place.

## Long description

Orca is built for freelancers, consultants, small agencies and service providers who today waste hours formatting proposals in Word or Google Docs.

**What you get:**

- 📄 **Proposals in 2 minutes** — add client, list items, generate link.
- 📱 **Works on mobile** — write it during the meeting, send it on the way home.
- 🎨 **Your brand, not ours** — logo, colors and visual identity from your company.
- ✍️ **Digital signature** — clients sign right on the link, no install.
- 📊 **See when clients open** — know the exact moment to follow up.
- 🔒 **Secure** — your data and your clients' data stays with you.

**Plans:**

- **Free**: 3 proposals to try it out
- **Pro**: unlimited proposals + your branding
- **Business**: everything in Pro + full Orca brand removal

Start free at [orca-mento.app](https://orca-mento.app).
